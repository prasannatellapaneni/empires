How to play

Goal: destroy the enemy Town Center in the far corner of the map before they destroy yours.

Select: left-click a unit, or drag a box around several. Order: right-click to move, gather (on trees, berries, gold), or attack an enemy.

Economy: villagers gather wood, food, and gold and return them to your Town Center — or to a Mill, Lumber Camp, or Mining Camp built near the resource to cut walking time. Select villagers and use the Gather buttons to put them to work fast. Train more villagers there. Build Farms when berries run out, and Houses to raise your population cap.

Military: units counter each other — spearmen shred cavalry, archers beat spearmen, knights ride down archers, and catapults crush buildings and massed troops. Build a Blacksmith (Feudal Age) for attack and armor upgrades, and research unit-line upgrades like Longswordsman at their buildings.

Buildings: build a Barracks for swordsmen and spearmen. Advance ages at the Town Center to unlock the Archery Range, Stable, and Siege Workshop — and stronger troops.

Camera: W A S D / arrows / screen edge to pan, mouse wheel to zoom, Q and E to rotate, middle-drag to pan. Click the minimap to jump.

Keys: S stop (with units selected) · F2 select all soldiers · . cycle idle villagers · Esc cancel/deselect · H help · M sound.

Rally: select a building and right-click the ground to set where new units go — rally your Town Center onto a resource and fresh villagers start gathering it immediately.

Scores: hold Tab for the live scorecard.

Groups: Shift+1–9 assigns selected units to a group, 1–9 recalls it, double-tap the digit to jump the camera there.

Chat: press Enter to open the chat line. Old soldiers speak of strange words with strange powers…

Touch: tap to select, drag to pan, pinch to zoom, long-press to order.
